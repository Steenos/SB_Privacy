## 2. Information We Collect

We do **not** require you to create an account, and we do **not** ask for your name, email, phone number, or address to play the game.

The app and the third-party services it uses may collect the following:

### a) Game progress and settings (stored on your device)
Your level progress, coins, gems, unlocked items, purchases, and preferences are saved **locally on your device** using your device's local storage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us. Deleting the app removes this data.

### b) Advertising data
We use **Google AdMob** to show rewarded video ads. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and process:

- Device identifiers (such as the advertising identifier / IDFA)
- General device information (device type, operating system, language, coarse location derived from IP)
- Ad interaction data (ads viewed, taps, rewards granted)

On iOS, the app shows Apple's **App Tracking Transparency (ATT)** prompt. If you do not allow tracking, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. In regions covered by GDPR, a consent prompt (Google UMP) is shown before ads load.

### c) Purchase data
In-app purchases (gem packs and bundles) are processed by **Apple** through the App Store / StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Apple may share limited transaction information needed to deliver and validate your purchase.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Sushi Go is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

If you intend to target this app to children, you must configure AdMob to serve child-directed, non-personalized ads and comply with COPPA / GDPR-K requirements.

## 6. Your Choices

- **Advertising / tracking:** You can change tracking permission at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking** (iOS), or reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.
- **Personalized ads:** Decline the ATT prompt or the consent prompt to receive non-personalized ads.
- **Local data:** Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored game data.
